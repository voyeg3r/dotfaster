# instalação

Para quem usa o vim-plug

    hg clone http://bitbucket.org/sjl/gundo.vim ~/.config/nvim/plugged/gundo

Add a mapping to your ~/.vimrc (change the key to suit your taste):

    nnoremap <F5> :GundoToggle<CR>
